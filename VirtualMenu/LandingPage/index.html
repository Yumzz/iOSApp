How Does Our Service Work?

Once a restaurant joins our community, a customer can then search for it on our app. With our easy to use interface, the customer can access the entire menu of the restaurant. As we are directly connected with the restaurant's ordering system, a customer can send an order straight to the restaurant from the app.

Why Use Yumzz?

As a restaurant, you get a free service that brings in more customers due to an elevated level/feeling of safety and gives you a technological edge over your competition. You also join a platform that is committed to strengthening loyalty between you and your customers by providing greater features over time at no cost to you. In order to get connected and onboarded, please contact us.

As a customer, there is no unneeded hassle of trying to figure out the new norms of dining out. With this app, you sit down, order, and your food comes when it is ready. This app also grants greater access to your waiter, which ultimately creates a a more seamless experience. Not to mention: it's free.
